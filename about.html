OUR HISTORY

Founded in 1998 as a specialized tool-and-die shop, Precision Steel has evolved into a tier-one fabrication facility. Our growth is mirrored in the complexity of the projects we undertake—from simple brackets to structural aerospace components.

1998 Founding in a 2,000 sq ft workshop.
2010 Expansion to automated CNC machining.
2024 Global leader in specialized fabrication.
